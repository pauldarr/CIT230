Site Name

silveroakshoa.org

Site Purpose Statement

This site will provide information about the community and Home Owners Association (HOA) to residents of the Silver Oaks community. This site may also be used as an informational piece for potential home owners seeking information about the community.

Target Audience

  • People living in the Silver Oaks community of San Antonio, TX.
  • Ages 18-70, male or female.
  • Secondary education to professional education.
  • Economic status may vary from low to middle income.
  • English proficiency is expected.
  • Possess technical knowledge to browse the web on computers or hand-held devices using Firefox, Safari, or another browser.
  • Concerned about community.
  • Seeking information about the community or HOA.

Scenarios

  1. A resident gets their HOA quarterly bill and wants to pay online. What site would they go to first?
  2. A homeowner has a neighbor that has let trash build up in their yard. Who would they contact to get the person to clean up their yard?
  3. A father wants to build a shed to house his lawn tools. Are there any rules and does he need to send anything to the HOA? Where would he find this information?
  4. A mother is interested if there are any mother's groups in the neighborhood. Who would she contact about that?

Content Architecture

Content List

How will the resident pay their HOA bill?

  • Provide a links page and FAQ.
  • Include links to the bill pay in both locations.

Who would they contact to have a homeowner clean their yard?

  • Include a violations page with information about common violations and how to report them.

Style Guide

Color Scheme

I chose a color scheme based on matching to the HOA's current logo. I used the GNU Image Manipulation Program (GIMP) to match the colors.

Background

The background simply uses #ffffff

Text

The text uses #000000 for legibility.

Headings

The headings use #747474 for a legible silver contrast.

Links

I prefer not to change link colors. It tends to confuse users on pages I have attempted that with.

After looking at the colors, I think the HOA was deeply inspired by the San Antonio Spurs for the color scheme.

Typography

Font Family

I chose Futura as the font to use through out my webpage. I looked at a list of the best looking modern fonts and this one looked nice without sticking out too much.

Sizing and Line Height

I used what was cited as average heading font size and line height according to other sources. It looked pretty good after I set it.

h1 font-size: 2em, line-height: 1.25em

h2 font-size: 1.5em, line-height: 1.2em

h3 font-size: 1.3em, line-height: 1.15em

h4 font-size: 1em, line-height: 1.10em

h5 font-size: 0.8em, line-height: 1.05em

h6 font-size: 0.7em, line-height: 1em
