News

If you believe your outdoor holiday decorating skills deserve a little fanfare, this is your chance to get the recognition you deserve! The criteria that will be judged include first impression, use of lights and decorations, and overall presentation. Each winner will receive a great prize. Judging will take place between Dec 16-18. Be sure to have your holiday lights on from 6:00 pm until 10:00 pm each night. The deadline to register is Dec 10. Please submit your entry to: pauldarr@gmail.com with your name and address in the message.

If you love looking at lights and decorations and would like to assist in judging, please let me know here or by email. The only requirements for judging are that your home is not in the competition, and you will look at all the homes entered before ranking your top three choices.

2015 contest winners:

  • 1st Place - 11819 Woodland Deer - Jeffrey & Jodi McCain
  • 2nd Place - 11931 Pure Silver - Chris Slaten
  • 3rd Place - 8347 Lazy Pebble - Shelly Breiten
