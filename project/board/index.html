Committees

Architectural Control Authority (ACA)
The responsibility of the Architectural Control Authority is to review Improvement Requests and insure the submitted improvement conforms to the Community’s recorded Declaration of Covenants, Conditions, and Restrictions (DCCR’s) and/or approved Architectural Standards and Guidelines.
ACA Design Standards
Members of the ACA are appointed by the Declarant during the Development Period.
Members of the ACA are kept confidential.

Beautification Committee
This AD Hoc committee was formed during the 2011 Annual Membership Meeting.
Chairperson: Paul Darr
The responsibility of the Beautification Committee is to organize volunteers to hand-held hose watering of plants in the community's common areas and entrances during water restrictions and the installation and removal of holiday decorations at the entrances to the subdivision.
Volunteers are needed.

Communications Committee
Chairperson: vacant
The responsibility of the Communications Committee is to form a network of communications among the entire Association, the Board, and Committees. This may include publications of a Community Newsletter, a Directory of Members, an internet web site, Community Alerts, or other forms of media.

Safety Committee
Chairperson: vacant
The responsibility of the Safety Committee is to advise and assist the Board in developing and carrying out programs to promote safety, deter speeding, crime prevention, organizing National Night Out Event, and Neighborhood Watch programs.

Welcome Committee
Chairperson: vacant
The responsibility of the Welcome Committee is to assist the Board in welcoming new residents to the community. Traditional projects include but not limited to reviewing welcome letters sent by management, acquiring and packaging welcome baskets and information to new residents.

Social Committee
Chairperson: Bryan Pearce
The responsibility of the Welcome/Social Committee is to assist the Board in the planning of social activities to promote community involvement for the benefit of all residents. Events may include community wide garage sales, assist with National Night Out, block parties, Holiday decorations, and other events.

Urban Wildlife Committee
Chairperson: Bryan Pearce
The responsibility of the Urban Wildlife Committee is to assist the Board by organizing volunteers to address concerns with dangeous wildlife in the community and present viable solutions to the Board of Directors.
